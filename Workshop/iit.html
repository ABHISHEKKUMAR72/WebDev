About IIT Delhi
IIT Delhi was established in 1961 as one of the Institutes of National Importance. Since then, it has grown to become a leader in education, research, and innovation.
IIT Delhi has produced numerous leaders in various fields, including technology, business, and research. It consistently ranks among the top engineering institutions globally.
IIT Delhi aims to expand its research capabilities, foster innovation, and maintain its status as a top-tier global educational institution.
